Quiche Maritime

Serves:
4
Type:
Main Course

Narrative Version

Ingredients

  • 2 medium tomatoes sliced and marinated in French Dressing
  • 1 pound of smoke fish fillets
  • 1 cup milk
  • 3 cups cooked rice
  • 2 tablespoons butter, melted
  • 3 eggs beaten
  • 1 cup grated Canadian Cheddar

Instructions

Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F. Marinate the sliced tomatoes in French Dressing so they are fully coated. Take the smoked fish fillets and cut them into pieces 2-3 inches long. Place all into a small baking dish and add milk. Bake for 20 minutes or until fish separates into flakes with a fork.

Drain and reserve ¾ cup of liquid for later.

Grease a pie pltae and make a crust combining the rice, melted butter and 1 egg. Turn the crust into the pie plate evenly.

Sprinkle the pie crust with half of the shredded Cheddar Cheese and then add slices of fish. Then, sprinkle the other half of the cheese. Combined the reserved poaching liquid with 2 eggs and pour into pie plate. Back for 30 min. Remove from oven and arrange the tomatoes around the edge, skin up. Bake for 10 more minutes until set. Garnish with chopped green onions or chives.

Segmented

Ingredients

  • 2 medium tomatoes sliced and marinated in French Dressing
  • 1 pound of smoke fish fillets
  • 1 cup milk
  • 3 cups cooked rice
  • 2 tablespoons butter, melted
  • 3 eggs beaten
  • 1 cup grated Canadian Cheddar

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
  2. Marinate tomatoes in French Dressing
  3. Cut fish into pieces 2-3 inches long and place in a shallow baking dish.
  4. Add milk and bake for 20 minutes.
  5. Drain to reserve ¾ cup of the liquid
  6. Make a crust by combining the rice, butter and beating in one egg.
  7. Grease a pie plate and turn crusted into the plate evenly over the bottom. Make sure sides and rim form a pie shell.
  8. Sprinkle shell with half of the cheese and arrange fish on top. Then sprinkle remainder of the cheese.
  9. Combine the reserved liquid with egg and create a base for the quiche. Pour into pie plate and bake for 30 minutes.
  10. Remove from oven and arrange tomatoes with the skin up around the outside. Return to oven and bake for 10 minutes.
  11. Garnish with chives or chopped green onions.

Step-By-Step

  • 2 medium tomatoes
  • ½ cup French Dressing
  • 1 pound smoke fish fillets
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 ½ cups of rice
  • 2 tablespoons of butter
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 block of Canadian Cheddar Cheese
  • Green Onions or Chives

Instructions

  1. Slice tomatoes into 6 wedges each
  2. Place tomato wedges in a bowl with French dressing to marinade
  3. Cut fish into slices of 2-3 inches long
  4. Place tomatoes in baking pan
  5. Place fish in baking pan
  6. Add milk to baking pan
  7. Heat oven to 350 degrees F
  8. Bake fish mixture for 20 min
  9. While fish is baking, boil rice until soft.
  10. Melt butter in a small saucepan
  11. Remove baking dish from oven and drain into bowl retaining at least ¾ of the mixture.
  12. Make rice crust. Place 3 cups of cooked rice and 2 tablespoons of melted butter in a bowl and mix.
  13. Beat 1 egg and whip it into the rice crust mixture.
  14. Spread rice crust mixture into a small pie plate. Make the bottom even and ensure top of the plate is reached.
  15. Grate ½ cup of cheese.
  16. Sprinkle ½ cup of grated cheese onto pie crust.
  17. Lay out fish slides in pie crust.
  18. Grate ½ cup of cheese.
  19. Sprinkle ½ cup of grated cheese onto pie topping.
  20. Beat 2 eggs.
  21. Mix 2 beaten eggs with reserved mixture from fish and tomato bake.
  22. Pour egg mixture into pie crush.
  23. Bake for 30 min at 30 degrees F
  24. Remove from oven.
  25. Place tomato wedges around the edge with skin up.
  26. Bake for 10 more min.
  27. Chop green onions or chives.
  28. Place green onions or chives on top of baked pie.
