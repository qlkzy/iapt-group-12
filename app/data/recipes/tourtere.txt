Tourtère
Recipe Type: Main Course
Servings: 4
Narrative Version:
Ingredients:
1 ½ pound of ground lean pork
1 small onion minced
½ bup of boiling water
1 garlic clove, chopped
1 ½ teaspoons salt
¼ teaspoon celery salt
¼ teaspoon of black pepper
¼ teaspoon sage
pinch of ground cloves

Instructions:
Cook over low heat in a large saucepan, stirring constantly until meat loses its red colour and about half of the liquid has evaporated.  cover and cook about 45 minutes longer.
Boil and mash potatoes and mix in with the meat and allow to cool.  Preheat oven to 450 degrees F.  Prepare psatry for 2-crust, 9 inch pie.  Roll out half and line a 9-inch pie plate.  Fill with cooled meat mixture.  Roll out remainder of dough and cover pie.  Flute and seal edges.  Slash top of crust.  Bake for 10 min and reduce heat to 350 degrees F and bake for 30-40 minutes.

Segmented Version:
Ingredients:
1 ½ pound of ground lean pork
1 small onion minced
½ bup of boiling water
1 garlic clove, chopped
1 ½ teaspoons salt
¼ teaspoon celery salt
¼ teaspoon of black pepper
¼ teaspoon sage
pinch of ground cloves
2 ½ cups of all purpose flour
¾ tsp salt
⅔ cup of cold unsalted butter, cubed
⅓ cup of cold lard, cubed
⅓ cup cold water

Instructions:
1. Retrieve a 3-quart saucepan and combine meat, water, spices and garlic.
2. Cook over low heat, stiring constantly until meat is brown and half the liquid is gone.
3. Cover and cook for about 45 minutes.
4. While cooking meat, prepare potatoes.  Boil and mash.
5. Mix the meat and potatoes and allow them to cool.
6. Preheat oven to 450 degrees F
7. Prepare crust for 2-crust, 9-inch pie.  Whisk the flour and salt.
8. Blend or cut in butter and lard until it is in coarse crumbs.
9. Drizzle with water, tossing a while with a fork until ragged dough forms, and adding 1 tablespoon more water if necessary.
10. Divide dough in half and shape into disks.  Wrap and chill in fridge for 30 min.
11. Roll out 1 dough into a 9-inch pie crust and line a pie plate.  Fill with the meat mixture.  Roll out and cover the pie with the second dough.
12. Bake at 450 degrees F for 10 minutes and reduce heat to 350 degrees F.  Bake for 30-40 more minutes.

19. While cooling, make pie crust.  Begin by filling mixing bowl with 2 ½ cups of flour and ¾ teaspoon of salt.
